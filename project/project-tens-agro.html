Time Series Forecasting of Agricultural Product Sales using LSTM

Deep learning-based sales forecasting model for agro businesses, built on real Bangladeshi data and published at IEEE ICCCNT 2023, IIT Delhi.

Overview

This project builds a multi-step LSTM time series model to forecast future sales of agricultural products. The goal was to help an agro company move from manual, analog sales records to a data-driven planning workflow. It later became the basis of the IEEE paper: ‚ÄúTime Series Forecasting of Agricultural Products Sale Using Deep Learning.‚Äù

Problem Statement

How can we leverage LSTM models to forecast agricultural product sales at a regional and product-specific level? The business needed to anticipate demand for different packet sizes and regions to reduce stock-outs and over-production.

Tech Stack

Python Pandas NumPy Matplotlib TensorFlow / Keras Jupyter / Colab

Highlights

  • Used a real-world dataset from a Bangladeshi agro company.
  • Built a multi-step LSTM model for forward sales prediction.
  • Final MAE: ‚âà 8339.66 (aggregated sales value).
  • Performed product-wise, region-wise, and packet-size trend visualization.
  • Used calendar/cyclical features (month encodings) to capture seasonality.
  • Aligned with supply chain and inventory planning use-cases.
