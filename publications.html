Time Series Forecasting of Agricultural Products Sale Using Deep Learning

IEEE 14th International Conference on Computing, Communication and Networking Technologies (ICCCNT), IIT Delhi, July 2023

Authors: Md. Touhidur Rahman, Mst. Eshita Khatun, Archina Mahmuda Asha, Mohammad Jahangir Alam

This paper proposes a deep learning approach to forecast agricultural product sales using Multi-Step LSTM models. It improves forecasting accuracy and helps optimize supply chain and production planning decisions.

DOI: 10.1109/ICCCNT56998.2023.10307538
Conference: IEEE ICCCNT 2023, IIT Delhi
